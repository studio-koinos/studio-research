# references/

Architecture research, technology deep dives, and tech comparisons.

## What goes here

- How a technology, pattern, or approach works in depth
- Comparisons of alternative approaches (without a single-product verdict)
- Research that agents reference during implementation decisions
- Deep dives triggered by /deep-research

## What does NOT go here

- Product evaluations with adopt/defer/reject verdicts → `evaluations/`
- Step-by-step instructions → `guides/`
- Broad market surveys → `landscapes/`

## Required sections

TL;DR, How It Works, Current State, Applicability to Our Stack, Key Sources

## Naming

`[topic-slug].md` — e.g., `agent-kb-structure.md`, `obsidian-knowledge-management.md`
