# evaluations/

Tool and product viability assessments. Answers "should we adopt X?"

## What goes here

- Assessments of specific tools, SDKs, APIs, or services against our stack
- Scout evaluation outputs
- Viability verdicts: adopt, defer, or reject with reasoning
- Single-product focus (multi-product comparisons go in `landscapes/`)

## What does NOT go here

- General technology explanations without a verdict → `references/`
- Multi-product surveys or category overviews → `landscapes/`
- Step-by-step setup instructions → `guides/`
- Anything no longer accurate → `archive/`

## Required sections

TL;DR, What It Is, Viability Assessment (adopt/defer/reject), Tradeoffs, Key Sources

## Naming

`[product-or-tool-slug].md` — e.g., `elevenlabs-voice-api.md`, `railway-cron.md`
